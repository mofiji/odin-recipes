French Toast

A classic breakfast that can be made with minimal ingredients. Its delicious and easy to make.

Who doesn't love freshly made French toast? Following this simple recipe you can recreate one of our favorite breakfast dishes.

The simple bread dipped in a egg mixture breakfast dish is a classic all around the world, known by different names in different parts of the world.

Ingredients
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 eggs
  • 1/2 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 pinch salt
  • 6 thick aged slices of bread
Cooking Instructions
  1. Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in milk. Whisk in eggs, vanilla extract, cinnamon and salt until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Soak bread slices in milk mixture until saturated.
  4. Working in batches, cook bread on the griddle or pan until golden brown on each side. Serve hot.
  5. Top with toppings of choice.
